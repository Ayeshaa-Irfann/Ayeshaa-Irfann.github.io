Dr. Elizabeth Blackwell

Dr. Elizabeth Blackwell was a British-born American physician and the first woman to receive a medical degree in the United States. She specialized in obstetrics and gynecology advocating for women's health and paving the way for other women to pursue careers in medicine. Her achievements have had a lasting impact on the field of medicine.

Dr. Anthony Fauci

Dr. Anthony Fauci is an American physician and immunologist. He is renowned for his expertise in infectious diseases and has played a prominent role in public health, particularly during the COVID-19 pandemic. He has served as the director of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases (NIAID) since 1984.

Dr. Sanjay Gupta

Dr. Sanjay Gupta is an American neurosurgeon and medical correspondent. He specializes in neurosurgery, with a particular focus on spinal cord regeneration and innovative surgical techniques. He is also a well-known medical journalist.
