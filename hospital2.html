About

Sharjah Kuwait Hospital provides variety of specialized health care services at a high level of efficiency and excellence. Those services are provided by highly experienced and qualified medical staff in the fields of comprehensive health care. These services include General surgery such as Bariatric surgery, Gastrointestinal surgery, Thoracic surgery, orthopedics, and Internal Medicine. In addition to an equipped Endoscopy Unit with the latest technologies.
